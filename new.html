3. A Puɔny Te̱e̱ Ji̠ Mi̠ Ci̠ Nhial Cak

1. A puɔny te̱e̱ Ji̠n mi̠ ci̠ nhial cak kɛnɛ piny, Cuŋku diaal tin gɔw kɛn nyuɔth kɛ Gɔɔydu. A puɔny te̱e̱ Ji̠n mi̠ ci̠ nhial cak kɛnɛ piny, mi̠ ci̠ nhial cak puɔny kɛɛliw ɛ Duŋdu.

Puɔny kɛɛliw ɛ Duŋdu Kuothdä min gɔaa, puɔny kɛɛliw ɛ Duŋdu. Nhök ɛ Duŋdu. Buɔ̱m ɛ Duŋdu. Gɔɔy ɛ Duŋdu Kuoth. ŋɔaani̠ diaal wi̠i̠ muɔ̱ɔ̱n kɛ Nyinku.

2. A puɔny te̱e̱ Ji̠n mi̠ ci̠ nhial cak kɛnɛ piny. Lätku diaal tin gɔw kɛn gääykɛ-ɛ naath. A puɔny te̱e̱ Ji̠n mi̠ ci̠ nhial cak kɛnɛ piny. Mi̠ ci̠ nhial cak puɔny kɛɛliw ɛ Duŋdu.

3. A puɔny te̱e̱ Ji̠n mi̠ ci̠ nhial cak kɛnɛ piny. Nhök kɔ̱cä lɔaacdu tëë thi̠n a thiɛl pek. A puɔny te̱e̱ Ji̠n mi̠ ci̠ nhial cak kɛnɛ piny. Mi̠ ci̠ nhial cak puɔny kɛɛliw ɛ Duŋdu.

4. A puɔny te̱e̱ Ji̠n mi̠ ci̠ nhial cak kɛnɛ piny. Ji̠n di̠tni̠ Ji̠n kä kɛn ŋɔaani̠ diaal. A puɔny te̱e̱ Ji̠n mi̠ ci̠ nhial cak kɛnɛ piny. Mi̠ ci̠ nhial cak puɔny kɛɛliw ɛ Duŋdu. "

About This Hymnal

The Nuer SDA Hymnal App is a digital worship companion designed to preserve and share the rich hymn traditions of the Nuer Seventh-day Adventist community. Built with care and authenticity, it brings together faith, culture, and technology in one accessible platform.

The Purpose

This app was created to make worship more immersive and inclusive for Nuer-speaking believers worldwide. Whether at home, in church, or on the go, the hymnal is always within reach—helping families, choirs, and congregations connect with God through music.

The Vision

The Nuer SDA Hymnal App is more than a digital songbook—it is a living archive of faith. Future updates will expand features, add more hymns, and strengthen community engagement, ensuring that the songs of worship continue to inspire generations to come.

We hope this resource is a blessing to you.

About Author

I am Gatbel Duop Chol, creator of the Nuer SDA Hymnal App. My mission is to preserve Nuer worship traditions through technology, making hymns accessible with audio, search, and responsive design for believers everywhere.
